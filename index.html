Sightseeing

One of the most famous places to visit in Boston is the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston. Opened in 1876, the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston boasts a collection of over 450,000 works of art ranging from Egyptian art to contemporary art. In particular, its collection of Japanese and Chinese art, including ukiyoe prints, is one of the best in the West.
